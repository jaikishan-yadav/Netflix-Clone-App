Frequently Asked Questions

  • Netflix is a subscription-based streaming service that allows our members to watch TV shows and movies on an internet-connected device.Depending on your plan, you can also download TV shows and movies to your iOS, Android, or Windows 10 device and watch without an internet connection.

  • Netflix offers three subscription plans: Standard with Ads: $6.99 per month Standard: $15.49 per month Premium: $22.99 per month In India, the plans are as follows: Mobile-only plan: ₹149 per month Basic plan: ₹199 per month Standard plan: ₹499 per month Premium plan: ₹649 per month The Standard with Ads plan is the cheapest, but it includes ads. The Standard plan is ad-free and allows you to watch on two screens at the same time. The Premium plan allows you to watch on four screens at the same time and includes Ultra HD video quality. You can add extra member slots to your Standard or Premium plan for $7.99 each per month in the US and ₹79 per month in India. Netflix prices may change in the future, so it's always best to check the Netflix website for the latest pricing information.

  • You can watch Netflix on any device with an internet connection, including: Smart TVs Streaming devices (e.g. Roku, Chromecast, Apple TV) Game consoles (e.g. PlayStation, Xbox) Mobile devices (e.g. smartphones, tablets) Computers To watch Netflix on your TV, you'll need to connect your TV to the internet and download the Netflix app. Once you've downloaded the app, you can sign in with your Netflix account and start watching.To watch Netflix on your mobile device or computer, you can simply download the Netflix app from the App Store or Google Play. Once you've downloaded the app, you can sign in with your Netflix account and start watching.You can also watch Netflix on the web by visiting the Netflix website. Simply sign in with your Netflix account and start watching.If you're not sure how to watch Netflix on your device, you can check the Netflix website for help.

  • You can also cancel your subscription through the Netflix mobile app by following these steps: Open the Netflix app and sign in to your account. Tap on the Profile icon in the top right corner of the screen. Tap on Manage Profiles. Tap on the profile that you want to cancel the subscription for. Tap on Cancel Membership. Tap on Confirm to cancel your subscription. Once you cancel your subscription, you will still be able to watch Netflix until the end of your billing cycle. You will not be able to watch Netflix after your billing cycle ends.If you change your mind and want to restart your subscription, you can do so at any time by going to the Netflix website or mobile app and clicking on the Restart Membership button.
